Fear and Hunger and Termina

Fear and hunger is an RPG 2d game made in RPG maker that has very cute and innocent contents on the ideas of the fear inside the dungeons which is the first game,The 2nd game Termina is also made with rpg maker set on a town in a version of europe is a town called Prehevil,The plot consisting of 14 people uncovering the mysteries of the town when the train broke down during their way to prehevil, they thought it was a conspiracy of an evil Military called Bremen Army,But they can't escape unless they uncover the truth about the Bremen Army or the choice to attack the other 14 people of the train until one is ultimately left in only under 3 days or else something bad happens to the contestants.

5 Reasons why this Game is my Favorite

  • The accurate depictions of disabalities and insecurities present in the game.
  • The lore of the Characters and their meaning in their base and infected form that's represented from both design and story.
  • The day and night cycle that progresses through the game which makes you regret something so significant like sleeping.
  • It's a Turn Based Game(i love HSR too).
  • The over exaggerated difficulty that's set in the game.
